FAQ - Brain Support Brigade

What services does Brain Support Brigade offer?
Brain Support Brigade offers counseling and support services tailored for students dealing with academic stress, personal challenges, and mental health issues.
How can I schedule a counseling session?
You can schedule a counseling session by contacting us through our website's booking form or by calling our office during business hours.
Is counseling confidential?
Yes, all counseling sessions are confidential. Your privacy is our priority.
Are there any fees for counseling services?
Brain Support Brigade offers free counseling services to students enrolled in our partner institutions. Additional fees may apply for non-partner institution students.
How can I contact Brain Support Brigade for more information?
You can reach out to us through our contact form on the website or by emailing us directly at info@brainsupportbrigade.com.
What kind of training do interns receive?
Interns receive mentorship, participate in therapeutic sessions, and attend workshops and seminars to enhance their skills and knowledge.
How can students apply for an internship?
Interested students can apply through our website by submitting an application form along with their academic credentials and a cover letter.
